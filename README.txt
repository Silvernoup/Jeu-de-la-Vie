# Jeu-de-la-Vie

Click on space at first screen to launch the game.

Click on cells to change their state.

"Reinitialiser" : Reset area of life
"Jouer" : Launch evolution
"Stop" : Stop evolution
"Quitter" : Quit

Launching evolution causes the 'Reinitialiser' and 'Quitter' buttons invalidity.
You can either click on 'Stop' button or press escape to end evolution.
